Included Software:

Architecture and 2D Drafting

This program covers essential skills for modern architecture and drafting, with hands-on projects that prepare you for creating 3D architectural models and 2D drafts using leading software tools.

Curriculum

  • Architecture Design & Rendering
    • 1. Introduction to 3D Modeling
      -- Using ARCHICAD for basic structure creation and design.
    • 2. Architectural Elements
      -- Walls, roofs, windows, and doors modeling.
    • 3. Rendering with Twinmotion
      -- Realistic rendering with lighting, materials, and animation.
    • 4. Final Project
      -- Complete design and rendering project.
  • Floor Planning and 2D Drafting
    • 1. Introduction to AutoCAD
      -- Familiarizing with AutoCAD’s interface and basic tools.
    • 2. Floor Plan Basics
      -- Creating layout, walls, and dimensions.
    • 3. Technical Drafting
      -- Detailing and annotations for technical clarity.
    • 4. Final Project
      -- Complete floor plan with detailed annotations.

Architecture & Drafting Modules

Whether you’re a beginner or looking to expand your skills, our courses provide comprehensive instruction in architecture & drafting essentials. You can also choose to study each software individually, focusing on the tools and skills most relevant to your personal goals and career aspirations.

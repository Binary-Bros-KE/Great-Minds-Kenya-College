Graphic Design Course

This course will help you master the skills necessary to excel in the world of graphic design. With hands-on projects and real-world exercises, you’ll develop expertise in Adobe Photoshop, Corel Draw, and Adobe Illustrator. By the end of this course, you'll be able to create a variety of professional-quality designs for personal and commercial purposes. Each software will equip you with a unique set of tools and techniques that make up a well-rounded designer's toolkit.

Curriculum

  • Basic Package
    • 1. Introduction to Design Principles
      -- Overview of color theory, composition, and visual balance.
    • 2. Typography Basics
      -- Learn how to effectively use fonts and typefaces to enhance readability and style.
    • 3. Basic Photo Editing
      --Develop foundational skills in photo enhancement, color correction, and retouching.
    • 4. Logo Design Essentials
      --Introduction to creating brand marks and logo design basics.
    • 5. Creating Social Media Graphics
      --Design eye-catching graphics tailored for social media platforms.
  • Professional Package
    • 1. Advanced Illustration Techniques
      --Deep dive into digital illustration for branding and identity design.
    • 2. Layout and Composition for Print and Digital
      --Learn to create layouts for both digital platforms and print media.
    • 3. Introduction to UI Design
      --Basics of designing for user interfaces, focusing on aesthetics and functionality.
    • 4. Building a Portfolio
      --Guidance on developing a professional portfolio that showcases your best work.
    • 5. Working with Clients
      --Understand how to manage client relationships and project timelines.

Graphic Design Modules

Whether you’re a beginner or looking to expand your skills, our courses provide comprehensive instruction in graphic design essentials. You can also choose to study each software individually, focusing on the tools and skills most relevant to your personal goals and career aspirations.
